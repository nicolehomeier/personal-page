PROJECT EXAMPLES

Some quick hits. Ask me about the who, why, and how!
Property-level Weather Reports

Summary of weather conditions at the property on a specific date or storm. These are used to give an adjuster a preview of what to expect when visiting the property, to confirm the date of loss or damage, or to document why damage from this peril is unlikely.

Weather and Home Safety iPhone App

Current, future, and past conditions in a clean interface. Weekly calendar of seasonal home maintenance tips. Property damage alerts and custom messaging from the Understory platform.

Agriculture Platform

Over a period of weeks, we built a platform to access current and historical weather information from Understory sensors. This platform was tailored to growers and included relevant weather information plus handy calculations such as GDDs and ET.

Solar Index

I created an algorithm to use battery voltage for a solar-powered device and use it to estimate daily solar irradiance or insolation. See it on display for the City of Somerville.

Blackout Risk Model

AER and Hartford Steam Boiler jointly developed a Blackout Risk Model to help companies understand their risk from power outage events. I led this development and worked with software engineers, scientists, and some great interns to develop proprietary data and models and bring the results to market.

Solar Storm Risk

We delivered a model evaluating blackout risk from geomagnetic storms. The audience for this two-year study was the commercial insurance market interested in evaluating losses from extended blackouts in North America. The report is proprietary and not publically released, but a diluted summary can be found here and parts of the model were published here.
